Key Takeaways

01January 2026 dominated every metric — 3.9M content views, 50K website sessions, 3,695 new leads, and $450,818 in booking revenue, the strongest single month in the period.
02Social following grew 91% from 77,207 to 147,127 — adding 69,920 net new followers at just $0.29 each, well below the $0.50 industry benchmark.
03$11,627 in ad spend generated $1,405,163 in total booking revenue — a 120× return on spend across the five-month period.
04Direct booking split averaged 76% and peaked at 85% in February 2026, up from 71% the prior year — reducing OTA fees and improving margin.
05Lead pipeline grew from 13,155 to 24,638 — an 11% period growth rate — at an average cost of $0.96 per lead, well below the $2 industry average.
